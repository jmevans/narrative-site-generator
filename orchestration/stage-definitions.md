# Stage Definitions

## Intake

Gather goals, audience, identity, projects, personal context, and media inventory.

## Story

Convert raw input into positioning, story units, proof gaps, and claims.

## Media

Review assets for story relevance, quality, privacy, and placement.

## Design

Choose visual identity and motion principles grounded in the story.

## Planning

Create sitemap, homepage structure, case studies, navigation, and CTAs.

## Copy

Write accurate, skimmable public-facing copy.

## Build

Generate or patch the website implementation.

## Review

Check truth, accessibility, privacy, performance, motion, and conversion.

## Refinement

Route user feedback to the smallest affected area and update only that area.
