# Story Patterns

## Builder

Someone who repeatedly turns ambiguity into working systems.

## Translator

Someone who connects technical, business, creative, or human contexts.

## Operator

Someone who improves messy real-world processes through judgment and execution.

## Explorer

Someone shaped by travel, risk, curiosity, or unconventional experiences.

## Craftsperson

Someone whose credibility comes from taste, precision, systems, and iteration.

Use these as interpretive patterns only. Do not force a user into a pattern without evidence.
