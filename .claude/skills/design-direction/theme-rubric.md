# Theme Rubric

Strong themes should be:

- Specific to the person's audience and story.
- Professional without feeling generic.
- Visually distinct but not gimmicky.
- Accessible on mobile and desktop.
- Compatible with the user's available media.

Avoid:

- Fake luxury aesthetics.
- Resume-template sameness.
- Decorative visuals unrelated to story.
- Overuse of gradients, glow, and giant slogans.
