# Section Patterns

## Hero

Identity, audience signal, primary proof, and CTA.

## Signature Work

Three to five projects or roles with clear outcomes.

## Case Study

Problem, role, constraints, approach, result, evidence.

## Operating Principles

Values translated into professional behavior.

## Life Lens

Personal material that makes the user memorable and human.

## Proof Wall

Metrics, testimonials, logos, artifacts, press, credentials, or shipped work.

## Contact CTA

Clear next step with low-friction contact path.
