# Asset Rubric

Score each asset from 0 to 5.

- 5: Strong story signal, strong visual quality, safe to use, clear placement.
- 4: Good asset with minor crop, caption, or quality needs.
- 3: Usable but needs context or enhancement.
- 2: Weak, generic, low quality, or unclear.
- 1: Significant quality, privacy, or relevance problem.
- 0: Do not use.

Recommended uses: hero, case_study, life_scene, process, gallery, background, transition, unused.
