# Media Analysis Skill

## Purpose

Review images, videos, screenshots, documents, and artifacts as story evidence and visual material.

## Evaluation Rubric

- Story relevance
- Professional relevance
- Emotional signal
- Visual quality
- Privacy or consent risk
- Best placement
- Crop/composition potential
- Motion potential

## Rules

- Do not identify people, places, or events unless provided by the user or visible with high confidence.
- Prefer assets that reveal process, environment, craft, or real experience.
- Exclude assets that are low quality, confusing, private, or unsupported by the story.
