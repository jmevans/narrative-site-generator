# Quality Gates

## Content

- Hero explains who the user is and why they matter.
- Claims are supported or marked as placeholders.
- Tone is specific, not generic.

## Design

- Palette matches intended emotion.
- Layout works on mobile.
- Contrast is accessible.

## Motion

- Animation supports story.
- Reduced-motion fallback exists.
- Motion is performant and restrained.

## Media

- Assets have clear purpose.
- Privacy risks are flagged.
- Low-quality assets are avoided or improved.

## Conversion

- CTA is clear.
- Contact path is easy.
- Page creates trust before asking for action.
