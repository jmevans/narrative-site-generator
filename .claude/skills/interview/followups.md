# Follow-Up Question Bank

## Career

- What was broken before you got involved?
- What did you personally contribute?
- What changed after the work shipped?
- Are there numbers that prove impact?
- Who benefited from the work?

## Projects

- Why did you build this?
- What tradeoffs did you make?
- What part are you most proud of?
- Do you have screenshots, demos, diagrams, or repos?

## Life

- What place or experience changed how you see the world?
- What challenge taught you something about yourself?
- What personal values show up in your work?
- Is this public-facing, private context, or something to ask before using?

## Media

- Which photos feel most like you?
- Which visuals show action, process, scale, or environment?
- Do any assets have privacy or consent concerns?
- Do you want the site to feel cinematic, editorial, technical, or product-focused?
