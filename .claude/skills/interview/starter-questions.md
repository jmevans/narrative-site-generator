# Starter Questions

1. What is the main goal of this website: get hired, attract clients, build authority, raise funding, tell your life story, or something else?
2. Who should the site impress most?
3. What should someone feel about you in the first 10 seconds?
4. What are 3-5 projects, roles, or achievements you are proudest of?
5. What personal experiences, adventures, travels, or challenges shaped you?
6. What photos, videos, screenshots, documents, or artifacts do you already have that could visually support the site?
