# Claim Rules

- Metrics require either a source, user confirmation, or placeholder labeling.
- Awards, titles, employers, schools, clients, and locations must not be invented.
- "Led", "owned", "founded", and "built" require role clarity.
- Claims about being first, best, largest, fastest, or most successful require strong proof.
- Personal stories must include visibility approval before becoming public copy.
- When in doubt, use specific modest language instead of broad superlatives.
